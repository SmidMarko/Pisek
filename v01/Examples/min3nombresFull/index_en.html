Min 3 numbers

Choose your programming language :

Statement

The aim of this exercise is to read 3 numbers on the input, and to output the minimum of these 3 numbers.

Limits

There are no other limits.

Input

Three numbers are given on the input, one per line, as the following example :

72
10
25

Ouput

The program must display on its output the smallest number, for exemple :

10
