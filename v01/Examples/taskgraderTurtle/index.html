Task Turtle

Statement

In this Python task, you must use a Turtle to go towards and stop at the coordinates given as input. Moreover, you must fill about 50% (+/-5%) of the resulting image in black.

Note that the Turtle starts at coordinates (0, 0) which is the center of the image; the image is 800x600 units wide.
